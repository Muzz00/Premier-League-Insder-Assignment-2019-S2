Introduction



The story is about the biggest and the most competitive football league in the world. The Premier League produces the most fascinating of Football matches where the competition places are decided by a mere few points.


The target audience for this website is Football Enthusiasts wanting to gain more knowledge and have an insight into the Premier League. Topics covered are the teams in race to win the league, European Champions League qualification, and the relegation scrap at the bottom of the Premier League.
