Maori Page



Football in the Maori Language can be said as whutupaoroa. Premier League in Maori can be translated as rūnanga.


A simple phrase such as "Football is the most watched and most played sport on earth" can be in Maori as "Ko te whutupaoro te tino hakinakina me te tino hakinakina i te whenua".
